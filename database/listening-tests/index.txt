Listening tests
===============

.. toctree::
    :maxdepth: 1

    human-label-file-format
    localisation
    coloration
    quality-ratings

In this part of the database we collect results from psychoacoustic experiments
run by the different labs involved in |twoears|. In addition to the results we
provide the underlying stimuli in a way that they can directly be fed into the
|BinSim| and provide the model with exactly the same audio input as the
listeners experienced during the actual experiments.
