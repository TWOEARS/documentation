================
Robotic platform
================

The goal of |TwoEars| is to provide an auditory model that is able to
interactively explore an acoustic scene. This can be achieved via the |BinSim|
which provides the model at its current state with the possibility to rotate its
head by applying different :abbr:`HRTF (head related transfer function)`\ s.
